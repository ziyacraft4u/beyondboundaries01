Match Duration

  • Each league match will consist of 12 overs per innings, with a 45-minute time restriction per innings.
  • Semi-finals and finals will consist of 16 overs per innings, with a 60-minute time restriction per innings.

Bowling Restrictions

  • A minimum of five bowlers must be used each match.
  • League Stage: Only one bowler may bowl a maximum of 3 overs.
  • Semi-final & Final: Only one bowler may bowl a maximum of 4 overs.

Powerplay Rules

  • 12-over match: 3-over powerplay — only 2 fielders allowed outside the 30-yard circle.
  • 16-over match: 4-over powerplay — only 2 fielders allowed outside the 30-yard circle.
  • After the powerplay, a maximum of 5 fielders are allowed outside the circle.

Player Eligibility

  • All players must be from the participating organization.
  • Emirates ID of all players will be verified by the match officials/organizing committee.
  • No outside players are allowed. If found, the team will be disqualified and the opponent declared the winner.

Impact Player Rule

  • The 12th man can be used as an Impact Player, following IPL regulations.
  • The Impact Player must be declared at the time of toss or be present on the ground at the toss.

Bowling Action (Chucking) & Umpire Authority

  • If a batsman suspects chucking, the umpire will review the next delivery; if found guilty it will be called a no-ball.
  • Repeated offense: the bowler will be barred from continuing the over and another bowler will complete it.
  • Umpires' decisions are final. Only the captain may discuss clarifications with the umpire; players are not allowed to argue.

Tournament Format

  • 8 teams divided into 2 groups of 4 teams each.
  • Top 2 teams from each group qualify for the semi-finals, followed by the final.
  • In case of a tie in league matches, a super over will decide the winner.
  • In case of equal points - Team with best NRR will be qualified to next level

Points System

  • Win: 2 points
  • Tie: Super Over
