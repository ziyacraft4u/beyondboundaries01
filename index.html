Key Tournament Details
  • Season: Season 01

  • Venue: SA Sports Cricket Ground, Ajman — Turf wickets and floodlights.

  • Digital Coverage: All matches scored online with live stats.
Prizes & Recognition
  • Champions: Crystal Trophy & Medals / Cash prize.
  • Runners-Up: Trophy & Medals / Cash prize.
  • Individual Awards: Man of the match, Orange Cap(Batsman), Purple Cap(Bowler).
  • Post-Event: Awards felicitation Ceremony.
